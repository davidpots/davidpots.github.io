## Verse chords

Here's the verse chords I play on ukulele. Broken into measures, the verse goes like this. You're repeating the same progression twice, and then slightly modifying it for the third play-through.

    "We're talking away..."
    | Em . . . | A . . . | D  . . . | G  . Bm . |

    "I'll say it anyway..."
    | Em . . . | A . . . | D  . . . | G  . Bm . |

    "Shying away..."
    | Em . . . | A . . . | Bm . . . | Bm . .  . |
